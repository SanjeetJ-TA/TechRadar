TECH RADAR

    Tech radar

    The Radar sets out the current view of approved technologies and practices within Digital and those that are of interest for the future.
    It is a knowledge base about where we are today as a team and where we want to go.

    Whats problems does the radar solve?

    It provides an open, easily accessible view of accepted practice within Digital for projects to use. Providing guidance on what technologies can, should or should not be used - as well as where Digital are looking for opportunities to see if technologies fit our use case to improve how we build technology.
    The original concept comes from thoughtworks, you can read more about it here.

    4 rings

    Adopt

    We have capacity to use these solutions, they have been through governance and are already been deployed in production.
    First choices to start a new project.

    Can Use

    Solutions that have demonstrated their usefulness. They have been through governance and are either in production or inflight. You can use it on your project but you need to provide a reason to not use the default choice.

    Assess

    These solutions may bring values, they have traction outside of our team, but it is not clear if these fit our use case. These technologies have not been through governance and would need to be assessed and test as part of governance approval for them to be used on a can use basis.

    Deprecated/ Rejected

    For various reasons, these solutions are no longer the first choices or are not recommended (anymore). They may be in production, and have been through governance, but new projects should not be using these technologies.
